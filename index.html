What is INDIA POST

INDIA POST is a digital property addressing system which ensures that all locations in the country are addressed. With this software, every location has a unique digital address. The digital address is a composite of the state code plus a generated digital code (region, district & area code). The software translates your GPS location to a user friendly digital address.

DIGITAL ADDRESS

The 12 digits address above is for the Sansad Marg, New Delhi, Delhi. In a deconstructed form,7JWV is the unique address within the postal code(approximately 100x100 meters). J6C7 is the unique address within the local region of postal area(approximately 14x14 meters). QM is more accurately describes the local area in 3x3 meters. The Postal Code and the Unique Address come together to form the unique digital address.
